> **Composition:** dynamic, **full-bleed, edge-to-edge**, **absolutely no border and no frame**. Aspect ratio 4:3 (use portrait 3:4 for a cover).

## 👑 Character Reference Sheet (keep consistent on every page)
*(In the Aranya Kanda the three live as forest-exiles, so they wear simple ascetic garments — bark-cloth and saffron — with hair in ascetic top-knots, yet keep their noble bearing and recognisable features.)*
- **Rama** — gentle prince, soft **blue/teal skin**, large kind eyes, calm brave half-smile; simple saffron/bark-cloth wrap, dark hair in an ascetic top-knot, golden quiver and great bow. Serene, radiating quiet strength.
- **Sita** — radiant golden-brown complexion, soft expressive eyes, jasmine flowers in a long dark braid; graceful simple ochre-and-saffron forest sari, minimal delicate gold jewelry. Gentle and devoted.
- **Lakshmana** — fair golden-brown skin, alert and protective; saffron bark-cloth, bow always ready; ever at Rama's side.
- **Ravana** — mighty **ten-headed demon-king of Lanka**, dark complexion, ten crowned heads (one dominant, others fanned behind), many powerful arms, ornate golden armour and jewels, deep red-and-gold robes, fierce eyes; imposing and regal but **never gory** — kid-friendly menace.
- **Jatayu** — majestic aged **giant eagle-king**, russet-and-gold plumage, broad sweeping wings, wise kind eyes, noble loyal bearing.
- **Shurpanakha** — Ravana's shape-shifting demoness sister; wild dark hair, sharp features, ragged crimson garments; menacing yet a touch comical, gentle and not frightening.
- **Golden Deer (Maricha in disguise)** — an enchanting magical fawn, shimmering golden coat with silver spots and jeweled antlers, big innocent eyes, a faint uncanny sparkle.
- **Sages (Agastya, Sharabhanga, Sutikshna)** — serene powerful ascetics, long white beards, soft glowing auras, saffron robes, *rudraksha* beads.
- **Shabari** — a frail, loving old woman ascetic, white hair, simple bark robes, a joyful wrinkled face full of devotion.
