## 🎨 Master Style Block (for visual consistency)
> **Medium & technique:** Traditional hand-painted **watercolour illustration** for a children's picture book — soft **wet-on-wet washes**, luminous translucent layers, delicate pigment **blooms and bleeds**, granular **cold-press paper texture**, fine pencil-and-ink linework, glowing white-paper highlights, dreamy soft edges, a little loose and hand-made.
> **Palette:** saffron, marigold gold, turquoise, rose-magenta, leaf green and deep indigo, with warm golden light.
> **Mood:** magical, gentle, wholesome, storybook-warm.
> **Format:** aspect ratio 4:3, full-bleed illustration.

## 👑 Character Reference Sheet (keep consistent on every page)
- **Rama** — gentle prince, soft **blue-tinted skin**, large kind lotus-shaped eyes, calm half-smile; golden-yellow silk *dhoti*, jeweled gold crown (*mukut*), pearl-and-emerald necklaces, golden armlets; carries a bow. Eldest, serene, radiating quiet light.
- **Lakshmana** — fair golden skin, alert and devoted; saffron-orange *dhoti*, simpler gold ornaments; always at Rama's side.
- **Sita** — radiant golden complexion, soft expressive eyes, jasmine flowers in long dark braid; red-and-gold *sari*, delicate gold jewelry (*maang tikka*, bangles, nose-ring). Graceful and gentle.
- **Vishwamitra** — tall elderly sage, long white beard, matted top-knot, weathered kind-but-fiery eyes; ochre-saffron robes, *rudraksha* bead strings, tall wooden staff.
- **Dasaratha** — aged noble king, grey beard, gentle tired eyes; cream-and-gold royal robes, tall jeweled crown.
- **Janaka** — wise silver-bearded king of Mithila, serene smile, elegant white-and-gold robes with pearl strands.
