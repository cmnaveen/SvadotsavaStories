## How to Use This Book
Each page has two parts:

## 🎨 Master Style Block (for visual consistency)
> **Medium & style:** Richly detailed **digital children's-storybook illustration**, in the style of a modern *Illustrated Ramayana for Children* cover — **semi-realistic yet softly stylized** characters with expressive friendly faces, large warm eyes and smooth skin; polished **digital painting** with gentle **cel-shading**, soft airbrushed gradients and crisp clean highlights; finely detailed costumes, fur, ornaments and architecture; **lush painterly backgrounds** with atmospheric depth and soft focus.
> **Lighting:** warm **cinematic** directional light with a gentle glowing rim-light and soft ambient glow.
> **Colour:** vibrant, saturated, harmonious — turquoise ocean blues, saffron, marigold gold, rose-magenta, royal crimson and deep indigo.
> **Skin tones:** Only **Rama** (and, where they appear, divine forms of **Vishnu** and **Shiva**) has gentle **blue/teal skin**; **every other character has a natural human skin tone** — warm golden-brown, fair or tawny — and is **never blue**. *Vanaras* (monkey-people) keep natural fur colours.
> **Costume:** strictly **ancient, traditional Indian attire** — *dhotis*, *angavastrams*, draped *saris*, *mukut* crowns and traditional gold jewelry, bark-cloth and deerskin for forest-exiles, classical robes and armour — with **absolutely no modern clothing, fabrics, footwear or accessories**.
> **Composition:** dynamic, **full-bleed, edge-to-edge**, **absolutely no border and no frame**. Aspect ratio 4:3 (use portrait 3:4 for a cover).

## 👑 Character Reference Sheet (keep consistent on every page)
- **Hanuman** — the mighty, devoted monkey-hero; golden-furred *vanara* with a kind, noble, intelligent face, expressive eyes, a long tail, a simple loincloth and a few gold ornaments. Can grow giant or shrink small; humble, brave and utterly loyal, with a soft heroic glow.
- **Sita** — radiant golden-brown complexion, jasmine in a long dark braid (now a little dimmed by sorrow), simple ochre-and-saffron sari, minimal gold jewelry; gentle, sorrowful but unshakably faithful and brave.
- **Ravana** — mighty **ten-headed demon-king of Lanka**, dark complexion, ten crowned heads (one dominant, others fanned behind), many powerful arms, ornate golden armour and crimson robes, fierce eyes; imposing and regal but **never gory** — kid-friendly menace.
- **Rama** — gentle prince, soft **blue/teal skin**, kind eyes; simple saffron exile dress, ascetic top-knot, great bow. (Appears at the start and the end.)
- **Lakshmana** — fair golden-brown skin, saffron exile dress, devoted; at Rama's side.
- **Vibhishana** — Ravana's good and noble younger brother, a dignified, kind-faced demon (rakshasa) in fine but gentler robes, with a wise, righteous air.
- **Trijata** — a kindly older demoness who is gentle to Sita; a softer, motherly rakshasi face.
