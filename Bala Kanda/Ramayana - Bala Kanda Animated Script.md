## DIRECTOR'S SCRIPTURAL CORRECTION NOTES

Four corrections from the authentic Valmiki text have been applied throughout this document:

1. **Garden meeting removed.** Rama and Sita do NOT meet in a palace garden before the wedding, nor does Sita pray to Goddess Gauri to win Rama. This scene exists only in later traditions (Tulsidas's *Ramcharitmanas*, Kambar's *Kamba Ramayanam*). In Valmiki, their first sight of each other is at the marriage pavilion after the bow is broken. Old Episode 17 (garden meeting) has been replaced with the canonical Episode 17 — the breaking of the bow.

2. **Parasurama relocated after the weddings.** In Valmiki's *Bala Kanda*, Sargas 74–76, the Parasurama confrontation occurs on the road back to Ayodhya, after all four weddings are complete. The weddings happen in Episodes 19–20; Parasurama appears in Episode 21.

3. **500 men for Shiva's bow, not 5,000.** Valmiki states explicitly in Sarga 67, Shloka 4: *pañcaśatāni ca nṛṇām* — "five hundred men." Corrected in Episode 16.

4. **Tataka's death is a slaying, not a spiritual ascent.** In Valmiki, Tataka is an irredeemable rakshasi who is killed to protect the sages. She does not transform into a beautiful spirit that ascends to heaven. The forest and sky simply rejoice at her defeat. Corrected in Episode 10.
