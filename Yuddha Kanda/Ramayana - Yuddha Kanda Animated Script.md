## DIRECTOR'S PRODUCTION NOTES — YUDDHA KANDA

This production script is designed to bring the Yuddha Kanda to life in a modern, 3D animated cinematic format. The visual style remains consistent with the Pixar-esque aesthetics used throughout the series. Every prompt is structured chronologically to guide the AI video generator.

## MASTER VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

All prompts in this document share the following base style. Paste these lines at the end of every scene prompt:

> **Animation Style:** 3D animated cinematic style, vibrant colors, Pixar-esque aesthetics, fluid character movement, kid-friendly, 24fps.
> **Lighting:** Warm cinematic directional light, gentle golden rim-light, soft ambient glow. Scene-specific lighting specified per prompt.
> **Skin Tones:** ONLY Rama has luminous blue/teal skin. Every other character has a natural human skin tone — warm golden-brown, fair, tawny, or deep brown.
> **Costume:** Strictly ancient traditional Indian attire — silk dhotis, bark-cloth for exiles, draped saris, mukuta crowns, traditional gold jewelry. No modern clothing.
> **Composition:** Dynamic full-bleed, edge-to-edge. No border, no frame. Rich painterly backgrounds.
